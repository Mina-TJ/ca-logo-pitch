My Logo Design

A Logo Built for Corporate Analytics

Every shape, every colour, and every line in this logo has a purpose. It tells the story of what Corporate Analytics does — turning complex data into clear decisions, for every part of JDI.

Designer: Mina T. Jouybari Role: Associate Data Analyst - Co-op Team: Corporate Analytics, J.D. Irving, Limited
Corporate Analytics

01 — The Design

What Does Each Part Mean?

I didn't pick these shapes randomly. Each element represents something real about what Corporate Analytics does every day at JDI.

🌐
The Globe
JDI operates across many industries — from forestry to transportation to consumer products. Corporate Analytics supports all of them. The globe shows that our work has no limits. We serve the whole business.
📊
The Bar Chart
The bars sit right at the center of the globe — because data is at the center of everything CA does. Each bar is taller than the last, showing we are always improving and always raising the standard.
↗️
The Rising Arrow
The arrow moves upward and forward. It says: we don't just look at past numbers — we use data to move the business forward. Analytics that lead to real action and better results.
🟢
The Green Colour
This is JDI's own green — the same colour on every Irving sign and truck. I used the colour that already belongs to this company, because this logo should feel like it has always been here.
