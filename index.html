Irving, Limited ￼ CORPORATE ANALYTICS ￼ 01. THE DESIGN What Does Each Part Mean? I didn't pick these shapes randomly. Each element represents something real about what Corporate Analytics does every day at JDI. 🌐 THE GLOBE JDI operates across many industries. From forestry to transportation to consumer products. Corporate Analytics supports all of them. The globe shows that our work has no limits. We serve the whole business. 📊 THE BAR CHART The bars sit right at the center of the globe. Because data is at the center of everything CA does. Each bar is taller than the last, showing we are always improving and always raising the standard. ↗️ THE RISING ARROW The arrow moves upward and forward. It says: we don't just look at past numbers. We use data to move the business forward. Analytics that lead to real action and better results. 🟢 THE GREEN COLOUR The logo uses a deep, rich green. A deliberate choice to give Corporate Analytics its own visual identity, while staying true to the JDI green family. Think of it as CA's own shade of Irving. ￼ 02. BRAND COLOURS Rooted in the JDI Green Family The logo colour was chosen from within JDI's green identity. A deeper, richer tone that gives Corporate Analytics its own presence while staying unmistakably part of the Irving brand. JDI Official Green #059666 CA Logo Green #1a6b35 Background #0d0d0d White #ffffff Why a slightly different green? The logo uses a deeper shade within JDI's green family. This was intentional. Just as different Irving divisions carry their own identity, Corporate Analytics has its own shade. Distinct enough to stand out. Close enough to always feel like home. ￼ 03.
