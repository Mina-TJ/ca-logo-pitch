01. The Design

What Does Each Part Mean?

I didn't pick these shapes randomly. Each element represents something real about what Corporate Analytics does every day at JDI.

01
Element One
The Globe
JDI operates across many industries. From forestry to transportation to consumer products. Corporate Analytics supports all of them. The globe shows that our work has no limits. We serve the whole business.
02
Element Two
The Bar Chart
The bars sit right at the center of the globe. Because data is at the center of everything CA does. Each bar is taller than the last, showing we are always improving and always raising the standard.
03
Element Three
The Rising Arrow
The arrow moves upward and forward. We don't just look at past numbers. We use data to move the business forward. Analytics that lead to real action and better results.
04
Element Four
The Green Colour
The logo uses a deep, rich green. A deliberate choice to give Corporate Analytics its own visual identity, while staying true to the JDI green family. Think of it as CA's own shade of Irving.
02. Brand Colours

Rooted in the JDI Green Family

The logo colour was chosen from within JDI's green identity. A deeper, richer tone that gives Corporate Analytics its own presence while staying unmistakably part of the Irving brand.
