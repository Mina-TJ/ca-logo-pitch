01. The Design

A Logo Built for Corporate Analytics

Every shape, every colour, and every line has a purpose. It tells the story of what Corporate Analytics does — turning complex data into clear decisions, for every part of JDI.

Corporate Analytics
01
Element One
The Globe
JDI operates across many industries. From forestry to transportation to consumer products. CA supports all of them. The globe shows our work has no limits.
02
Element Two
The Bar Chart
Bars sit at the center of the globe because data is at the center of everything CA does. Each bar is taller than the last, showing continuous improvement.
03
Element Three
The Rising Arrow
The arrow moves upward and forward. We do not just look at past numbers. We use data to move the business forward. Analytics that lead to real action.
04
Element Four
The Orbit Ring
CA does not just support JDI divisions. It wraps around them. The orbit ring shows connection and movement, with Corporate Analytics always surrounding the data and the teams.
05
Element Five
The Green Colour
CA Logo Green (#1a6b35) is a deeper shade than JDI Official Green (#059666). Each Irving division carries its own identity while belonging to the same family. This colour gives Corporate Analytics its own presence within the Irving brand.
